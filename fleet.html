Here at LOT Virtual, the fleet size is amazing. You have the choice to either go short haul, medium haul or even long haul. Take a look at our fleet below!

Boeing 737-800

The Boeing 737 family is one of the most successful aircraft families in aviation history. LOT Virtual uses the Boeing 737-800 for medium-haul international flights, such as Warsaw to Tbilisi, Warsaw to Yerevan and other similar routes.

Embraer E170

LOT Polish Airlines is proudly the launch customer of the E170. LOT Polish Airlines operates the entire E-Jet series, which ranges from the E170 to the E195. Our E170 is used for most of our European international flights, as well as in some regional hops.

Boeing 787-8

Also known as the "Dreamliner", the Boeing 787 is one of the most modern and efficient aircraft of todayâ€™s times. LOT Virtual uses the 787 for our long-haul international flights.

Dash 8 Q-400

The Dash 8 Q400 by Bombardier is the smallest aircraft in our fleet. It carries about 80 passengers. We use the Q400 for many of our short, regional flights from our main hub Warsaw and other regional bases.

Embreaer E190

The Embraer E195 is the bigger brother of the E170; it carries more passengers and has more range. We use our E195s for longer European flights, such as Warsaw to Istanbul, in addition to serving the same European routes alongside the E170.
