Destinations

We have tons of beatiful destinations ready for yo to discover. But here are two of our most incredible locations!

Lublin

Having been vastly developed during the times of the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth due its strategic trade location, Lublin has retained the unique architecture of that former commonwealth. The National Museum of Lublin is one of the oldest and largest museums in Eastern Poland, and its historical city center and squares add to that heritage. Lublin also hosts various concerts and festivals each year.

Nur-Sultan

Formerly known as Astana, the Kazakh capital has a lot to offer, from remarkable monuments such as Baiterek (which itself is a very popular tourist attraction), futuristic skyscrapers, The Palace of Peace and Reconciliation, and its many glamorous mosques.

Baku

Azerbaijan's capital Baku not only hosts a long coastline along the Caspian Sea, but is home to many forts and castles along its area, and a notable UNESCO World Heritage Site: Maiden Tower. Azerbaijan and the neighboring Caucuses countries have beautiful landscape, which makes flying here so special.
